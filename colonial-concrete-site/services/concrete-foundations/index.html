Concrete Foundations in Chicago, IL

The foundation is the one part of a build you can't afford to get wrong. Colonial Concrete & Demolition Co. pours footings, foundation walls, basement walls and structural concrete for residential and commercial projects across Chicago and DuPage County — to code, to spec, and built to carry the load.

Home › Services › Concrete Foundations

Footings & foundation walls

We form and pour footings sized to the load and the soil, then build foundation and basement walls that are straight, plumb and properly reinforced. Getting the footings right — depth, width, rebar — is what keeps everything above them stable for the life of the structure.

We pour below the frost line and detail for drainage and waterproofing so Chicago's water table and freeze-thaw cycles don't go to work on your foundation.

Structural pours & slabs

Beyond walls, we handle structural slabs, garage slabs, and the flatwork that ties a foundation package together. Every pour is formed, reinforced and finished to the engineering on the project.

Because we also do demolition and concrete removal in-house, we can clear and prep a site and pour the new foundation without juggling multiple contractors.

To code, inspected, done right

Foundation work is inspected work, and it should be. We build to the prints and the local code, and we coordinate the inspections that go with it. It's the part of the job nobody sees — which is exactly why it has to be done right.

Frequently asked questions

Do you pour foundations to code?
Yes. We build to the project prints and local code, pour below the frost line, and coordinate the required inspections for residential and commercial foundations.
Can you remove an old foundation and pour a new one?
Yes. Because we do demolition and concrete removal in-house, we can break out and haul off the old foundation and pour the new one without bringing in a separate contractor.
What foundation work do you handle?
Footings, foundation and basement walls, structural slabs and garage slabs for both new builds and replacements.
